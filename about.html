About Us

HANDOURA Import Export

We are a dynamic player in global trade, specializing in the import, export, and distribution of fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG). We also provide comprehensive logistics solutions — including freight forwarding, transportation, and specialized services for diplomatic corps and international trade missions. With a commitment to excellence, we continuously evolve to keep pace with the ever-changing global market and rising demands in logistics and transportation. Our philosophy is rooted in delivering consistent quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. At Handoura, every service is tailored to meet client needs — anytime, anywhere. We aim to make trade seamless, offering end-to-end support so our customers can focus on growing their business while we take care of the logistics. With Handoura, you can trust that your goods are in capable hands — from origin to destination.
